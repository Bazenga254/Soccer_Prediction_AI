## Detailed Description
Spark AI brings AI-powered soccer match predictions directly to your favorite betting sites. No more switching tabs — get instant analysis overlays while you browse matches.

**What Spark AI Does:**

Get AI match predictions overlaid on 12+ supported betting sites:
- Betika, SportPesa, Mozzartbet, 1xBet (Kenya)
- Bet365, William Hill, Betway (International)
- DraftKings, FanDuel, BetMGM (US)

**Key Features:**

Match Analysis Panel
- AI-generated win/draw/loss probabilities
- Head-to-head history and recent form
- Key player impact ratings
- Injury and suspension reports

Live Score Tracking
- Track any match with one click
- Goal notifications with celebration animations
- Real-time score updates in the extension popup

Odds Comparison
- See odds from multiple bookmakers side by side
- Identify value bets where AI disagrees with the market
- Kelly criterion stake suggestions

**Covers 40+ Leagues Worldwide:**
Premier League, La Liga, Bundesliga, Serie A, Ligue 1, Champions League, Kenyan Premier League, Egyptian Premier League, MLS, Brazilian Serie A, J-League, and many more.

**How It Works:**
1. Install the extension
2. Sign in with Google or email
3. Visit any supported betting site
4. Click "Analyze" on any match to see AI predictions
5. Track matches for live goal notifications

**Free Features:**
- 3 free predictions daily
- Live score tracking
- Basic match analysis

**Pro Features:**
- Unlimited AI predictions
- Advanced player impact analysis
- Odds comparison across bookmakers
- Priority support
